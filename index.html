About me

Hi, I'm Gabby.

I'm a Data & AI Engineer with 6+ years of experience across finance, tech, and telecom — turning complex data problems into elegant, automated solutions. My career has spanned financial analysis, data analytics, product analysis, data development, and AI engineering, giving me a rare end-to-end perspective on how data actually moves through an organization.

My sweet spot is at the intersection of data engineering and AI — designing systems that don't just analyze what happened, but actively do something about it. I bring the rigor of a finance background, the technical depth of a data engineer, and the product instincts to know what actually matters to the business.

Based in Denver, CO. When I'm not building pipelines, I'm probably analyzing apartment prices or finding new ways to make data fun.
